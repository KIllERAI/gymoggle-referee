How to Play

1
Pick your battle

Choose squats, push-ups, jacks, or Plank Chicken. Then tap Find Opponent for a random match, or Create Game to get a code and play a friend.

2
Set up your camera

Prop your phone or laptop so your whole body is in frame. Stand back — the camera needs to see you move. Good lighting helps a lot.

3
Do the reps

The camera counts automatically — no tapping. Squat, push, or jump and watch your number climb. Most reps in the time limit wins.

4
Plank Chicken 🧱

A hold battle — both get into a plank, then it's a staredown. First one to drop loses. No timer, pure willpower.

5
Daily tasks & leaderboard

Come back daily for 3 fresh tasks and coins. Climb the leaderboard by winning matches and hitting personal bests.

💡 Tips
  • Face the camera straight-on for push-ups and planks.
  • Full range of motion — half-reps may not count.
  • Playing a friend? Trash talk in the lobby chat first. 😤
  • Your camera never leaves your device — only your rep count is sent.
